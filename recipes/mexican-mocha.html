Mexican Mocha

Description

This delectable treat is a must have for someone who likes both sweet and spicy!!! I personally enjoy this in the cooler months of Fall and Winter. Add this to your list, fellow coffee lovers! This is one of my easier recipes.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces strong coffee or two shots expresso
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1-3 tbsp heavy cream or half & half

Steps

  1. Make your coffee or espresso and put it in your mug of choosing.
  2. Mix your dry ingredients in a small bowl or cup.
  3. Add dry ingredients to your drink and stir
  4. Stir in heavy cream to your taste. Enjoy!!
  5. BONUS: Add whip cream to top
